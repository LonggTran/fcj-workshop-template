### Week 1 Objectives:

* Connect and become familiar with the members of First Cloud AI Journey, and understand the organization's regulations.
* Study the cloud resource ecosystem and AWS core services.
* Learn about the promotional account operating model and the platform's financial risk management mechanisms.
* Study the methodology for configuring AWS Budgets to proactively control system usage costs.
* Explore the AWS Support Center and understand the professional technical incident handling process.
* Study the Spec-Driven Development (SDD) software development methodology through the Kiro ecosystem.

### Week 1 Achievements:

* Built a network of connections and improved interaction effectiveness within the working environment:
  * Established effective methods for connecting, communicating, and learning from experts and colleagues participating in the FCAJ program.
  * Gained a thorough understanding of and strictly complied with professional working practices, rules, and disciplinary requirements at the internship organization.

* Understood the cloud ecosystem and the architecture of AWS core service categories:
  * Studied the AWS Console graphical interface and methods for accessing and categorizing major resource groups.
  * *Compute & Storage:* Mastered the virtualization principles of EC2 servers for application execution and durable object storage solutions for static data on Amazon S3.
  * *Networking & Database:* Understood the concept of isolated network segmentation through VPC/Subnets and the mechanism for managing highly available relational database systems through RDS.
  * *Security & Monitoring:* Explored the least-privilege authorization model using IAM policies and the principles by which CloudWatch collects system operational metrics.

* Mastered financial control and budget risk management methodologies with AWS Budgets:
  * Understood the technical nature, usage quota distribution conditions, and identity mechanisms of cloud account systems.
  * Studied in depth the operation of four budget control types: Cost Budget, Usage Budget, RI Budget, and Savings Plans Budget.
  * Analyzed the use of quick-start Budget Templates and methodologies for configuring intelligent alert thresholds to prevent unintended financial losses.

* Understood the technical support system and incident remediation process with AWS Support:
  * Clearly distinguished the service scope and technical benefits of different Support Plans to identify an appropriate recovery solution for each project scale.
  * Mastered Severity Level classification standards and the process of creating and closely tracking Support Cases to optimize interaction time with AWS engineers.

* Studied source-code automation concepts and next-generation software development with Kiro SDD:
  * Understood the core roles of Spec, Steering, and Agent Hooks in establishing a "single source of truth," enabling AI to understand and implement source code accurately according to system business requirements.
  * Analyzed the process of decomposing technical designs, creating sample configuration documents, and operating the Kiro CLI to orchestrate AI Agents for the automated construction of consistent application structures.
